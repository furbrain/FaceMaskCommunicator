Face Mask Communicator works by transcribing the users speech into text
and sends it over bluetooth to the selected device. The app uses android
SpeechRecognizer API to translate the users speech, and the audio data 
may be sent to Google and is covered by their privacy policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

No information is otherwise collected or stored. Underwood Underground
does and will not hold, process, or otherwise use *any* data from the user
